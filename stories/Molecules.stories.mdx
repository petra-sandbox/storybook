# Molecules
 
Grouping atoms together, such as combining a button, input and form label to build functionality.

### About atomic design
Atomic design is a methodology for crafting design systems with five fundamental building blocks, which, when combined, promote consistency, modularity, and scalability.

The five distinct levels of atomic design:
 - atoms
 - <b>molecules</b>
 - organisms
 - templates
 - pages
